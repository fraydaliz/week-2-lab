Popular Sports

Soccer

Soccer, also known as football in most countries,s the most popular sport in the world. It’s played by two teams of 11 players each, aiming to score goals by kicking a ball into the opposing team’s net.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport where two teams of five players aim to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop elevated 10 feet above the ground.

Tennis

Tennis is an individual or doubles sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net. Players use racquets to hit a ball over the net and aim to win points by making the ball land in the opponent’s court.
